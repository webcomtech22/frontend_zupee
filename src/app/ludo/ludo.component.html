HOW TO PLAY LUDO?

Ludo is a classic board game played by 2 to 4 players. Each player has four tokens that need to navigate around the board and reach the center. Players take turns rolling a dice to determine the number of spaces they can move their tokens. The first player to bring all their tokens to the center wins. Based on the dice number, move your token toward the home area. You don’t need to roll a 6 to start the game. Just tap on the token you want to take forward.

You get extra moves on throwing a 6 on Ludo Supreme and Ludo Supreme League. For all four zupee ludo games, you get an extra move for taking the token home and capturing the token on board.
